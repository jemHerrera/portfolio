    About BlackJack Game

    Blackjack is a game generally played in the Casino using the standard deck of cards. Also known as Twenty one, the objective of the player is to win money by creating card totals higher than those of the dealer's hand but not bust(exceed 21), or by stopping at a total in the hope that the dealer will bust.

    Features

    The following are the main features of the app:

    • Hit, Stand and Double-down player actions.
    • Smooth animations for a more ergonomic playing experience
    • Available in all screen sizes, including tablet and mobile

    Future Plans

    For the next phase of the project, I am planning to include the split action. Split is somewhat more complex that it creates another player entity that has its own hit, stand or double-down actions.
